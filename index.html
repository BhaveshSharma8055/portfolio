About Me

Hi, I'm Bhavesh Sharma. I'm a copywriter, cinephile, cricket fanatic, and someone who believes a push notification can change your day. I don't have agency experience (yet), but I've made memes that got reposted, ads that don't exist (but should), and I'm trying to turn "just ideas" into my reality.

But if you read even one thing here and smile, think, or say "damn, that's clever" then maybe I'm already doing the job. I write words that act like people. They flirt, argue, persuade, and vibe.

This portfolio isn't just about my work. It's about how I think. I love ads that make you pause. Push notifications that feel like a friend. Movies that feel like therapy.

If you're looking for fresh ideas, sharp copy, and a mind that's always cooking, we should talk.
